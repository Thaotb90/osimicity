the story

OF METROPOLIT

The city of Metropolit is a futuristic city in the year 2101 that has been largely taken over by big corporations after a technological revolution.

These corporations have taken control over a wide range of areas, such as weapons, robotics, pharmaceuticals, and communications and most of them operate above the law.

This has pushed many individuals with no family fortune nor corporate connections into the streets with no way of getting a decent job.

Rich is one of these many “street kids” of the Metropolit city, he doesn't have much himself but he is a dreamer and has ambitions of getting up the street ladder.

But as no one can achieve much alone, Rich needs to find and recruit more people with the same vision of standing up against the control of the “Korpo world”, and as everyone well knows, the greater your crew the higher your social status gets.

Rich is a racer, he is always the first and he is a fighter, which can sometimes get him into trouble but thanks to his charisma many people just seem to turn to his side, to join his gang, to fight alongside him very easily.

But of course, not everyone is that easily influenced by the charm of a guy in a well-fitted suit, some need a different type of persuasion methods ...

As the street culture in some of the Metropolit's avenues evolved into pretty much strength-based anarchy where the most respected is the guy or gal who can fight the best. Luckily Rich is no quitter and as a proper street kid, he also has some nice attacks up his sleeve.

So he started with this practice of fighting the best of the street fighters until they don't agree to join his gang and help him on his mission.
